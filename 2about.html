OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To promote full and equal social participation among youth
  • To build life skills among youths
  • To educate youths and the community in areas like HIV/AIDS, health, rights, food, gender and environment
  • To fight discrimination against HIV/AIDS infected, orphans, elderly and chronically ill
  • To educate and assist youths with drug and substance abuse challenges
